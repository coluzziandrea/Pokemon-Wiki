Welcome
to the Pokémon Wiki!

Pokémon Wiki is a wiki dedicated to the Pokémon Universe created by Satoshi Tajiri.

It centers on imaginary creatures called "Pokémon" that humans can capture, train and fight for fun.

The franchise was born as a pair of video games developed by Game Freak and published by Nintendo for the Game Boy handheld console.

The huge success achieved around the world has led to the creation of more video games, anime, movies, manga, a trading card game, books and countless gadgets and toys.

Our goal is to create a comprehensive encyclopedia for all official material related to this series.

This content comes from Poké API, and must be attributed to its authors, as specified in the license.
